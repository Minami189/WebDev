Ensuring Safety and Prioritizing Your Health


At Campus Crave, your safety is our top priority. We follow strict food handling procedures and hygiene protocols to ensure all meals are fresh, safe, and prepared with care.

How We Evaluate Food Vendors


Our goal is to create a safe and trustworthy platform where students can explore food options without worrying about health risks. We avoid recommending food businesses with a history of poor sanitation, expired ingredients, or non-compliance with local health regulations. We believe that great food should always come with peace of mind.

Allergen Information


Food allergies can be serious, and at Campus Crave, we do our best to recommend food vendors who label their menu items and take allergen safety as a top priority. We prioritize vendors to recommend who are mindful of allergens such as peanuts, dairy, eggs, shellfish, soy, wheat, gluten and more. We encourage all customers with food sensitivities to ask vendors about ingredients and preparation methods before ordering.
